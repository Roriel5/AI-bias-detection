How It Works

1

Upload

Upload a CSV with your model's ground truth labels, predictions, and sensitive attributes (e.g. gender, race).

2

Analyze

We compute Demographic Parity, Equalized Odds, and Disparate Impact across every group — including intersectional analysis.

3

Explain

Gemini AI translates the numbers into plain-language explanations, identifies root causes, and suggests ranked fixes.

4

Report

Download a professional PDF report card to share with stakeholders, compliance teams, or auditors.

What the Metrics Mean

Metric What It Measures Fair Range
Demographic Parity Difference Difference in positive prediction rates across groups < 0.05
Equalized Odds Difference Max difference in TPR and FPR across groups < 0.05
Disparate Impact Ratio Ratio of min/max positive prediction rates > 0.8
